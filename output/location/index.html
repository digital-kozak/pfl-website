Travel Info

Getting Here

By Air

Durango-La Plata County Airport (DRO) — 1 hour drive
Albuquerque International Sunport (ABQ) — 3.5 hours drive
Denver International Airport (DEN) — 5.5 hours drive

We recommend renting a car for the most flexibility during your stay.

By Car

From Denver: Take US-285 S to US-160 W (5.5 hours)
From Albuquerque: Take US-550 N to US-64 W (3.5 hours)
From Colorado Springs: Take US-115 S to US-160 W (4 hours)

All-wheel drive recommended in winter months.
