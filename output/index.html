Welcome

Where Comfort Meets
Adventure

Nestled in the San Juan Mountains, Pagosa Forest Lodge offers an unparalleled mountain retreat experience. Our thoughtfully designed cabin combines rustic elegance with modern amenities, creating the perfect sanctuary for your Colorado getaway.

Spacious Accommodations

4 beautifully appointed bedrooms sleeping up to 8 guests. Each room features premium bedding, mountain views, and thoughtful touches.

Premium Amenities

Relax in our private hot tub, cozy up by the fireplace, or enjoy panoramic mountain views from the expansive deck.

Prime Location

Just 40 minutes from Wolf Creek Ski Resort and close to Pagosa Springs' famous hot springs, hiking trails, and downtown charm.
