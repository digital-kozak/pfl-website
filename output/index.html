Welcome

Where Comfort Meets
Adventure

Nestled in the San Juan Mountains on 5 private acres, Pagosa Forest Lodge offers an unparalleled mountain retreat experience. Our secluded cabin backs directly onto the San Juan National Forest—step out your door and onto miles of pristine hiking trails.

Great place to unplug, but with all the modern conveniences. Whether you're gathering around the fire pit for s'mores, soaking in the hot tub under starry skies, or letting your dogs explore the wildlife-rich surroundings, this is your private forest sanctuary.

Spacious Accommodations

4 beautifully appointed bedrooms sleeping up to 8 guests. Each room features premium bedding, mountain views, and thoughtful touches.

Premium Amenities

Relax in our private hot tub, cozy up by the fireplace, or enjoy panoramic mountain views from the expansive deck.

National Forest Access

Step out your back door directly into the San Juan National Forest. Miles of secluded hiking trails, wildlife viewing, and untouched wilderness await—no driving required.
