Things To Do

Your Guide

Experience the Best of
Pagosa Springs

Nestled in the heart of the San Juan Mountains, Pagosa Springs offers year-round adventures for every type of traveler. From legendary powder skiing to soothing natural hot springs, world-class fishing to breathtaking hiking trails—your mountain escape is just the beginning.

Winter Adventures

20 Minutes • Nov-Apr

Wolf Creek Ski Resort

"The Most Snow in Colorado" averaging 430+ inches annually. Famous for its powder on the Alberta side, including the legendary Knife Ridge Chutes. Get there early on powder days—it fills by 9:30 AM!

  • 1,600 skiable acres
  • All skill levels welcome
  • Affordable lift tickets
5-30 Minutes • Dec-Mar

Snowshoeing & Cross-Country

Explore pristine winter landscapes on snowshoes or cross-country skis. The Piedra River Trail and Wolf Creek Trail offer groomed paths and backcountry adventures for all levels.

  • Equipment rentals available
  • Guided tours offered
  • Free trails in town
15 Minutes • Year-Round

The Springs Resort & Spa

Home to the world's deepest geothermal hot spring, The Springs Resort offers 24 pools ranging from 83°F to 114°F. Soak in mineral-rich waters while enjoying panoramic views of the San Juan Mountains—truly magical in winter when snow falls around you.

Summer Adventures

5-45 Minutes • May-Oct

Hiking & Trails

From the Continental Divide Trail to local favorites like Treasure Falls and Fourmile Falls. Wildflower meadows, alpine lakes, and breathtaking vistas await at every turn.

10-30 Minutes • Year-Round

Fishing Paradise

The San Juan River is legendary for trout fishing. Guided trips available for beginners and experts alike. Lake fishing at nearby Echo Lake and Williams Creek Reservoir.

20-60 Minutes • May-Sep

Rafting & Kayaking

From gentle family floats on the San Juan to adrenaline-pumping whitewater on the Piedra River. Local outfitters provide all equipment and guides.

5-30 Minutes • May-Oct

Mountain Biking

Miles of singletrack and forest roads for all skill levels. The Turkey Springs Trail System offers flowy rides with stunning views.

15 Minutes • May-Oct

Horseback Riding

Experience the Old West on guided trail rides through pristine wilderness. Perfect for families and romantic sunset rides.

At the Cabin • Year-Round

Stargazing

With minimal light pollution, Pagosa offers incredible dark sky viewing. See the Milky Way from our deck—no telescope needed!

Beyond the Outdoors

Local Attractions
& Dining

Downtown Pagosa

Charming main street with local breweries, art galleries, boutique shopping, and the historic Springs Resort. Great for a leisurely afternoon stroll.

Local Dining

From farm-to-table cuisine at The Alley House to craft brews at Pagosa Brewing, experience the best of Colorado's culinary scene.

Chimney Rock

Ancient ancestral Puebloan ruins perched on a dramatic sandstone butte. Guided tours reveal the mysteries of this 1,000-year-old ceremonial site.
