### 3. ü•æ Hike to Treasure Falls

**Best for:** Ages 3+ | Spring through Fall

This **1-mile round-trip hike** is the perfect introduction to Colorado hiking for kids. The trail is well-maintained with a gentle grade, and the payoff ‚Äî a stunning 105-foot waterfall ‚Äî is worth every step.

**Trail details:**
- Distance: 0.5 miles each way
- Elevation gain: ~200 feet
- Time: 30-45 minutes round trip
- Location: 25 minutes from the cabin on Hwy 160

**Kid tip:** Bring a picnic lunch to enjoy at the viewing platform.

**Cost:** FREE

### 4. üõ∑ Go Sledding at Reservoir Hill

**Best for:** Ages 2+ | Winter season

Located right in town, **Reservoir Hill** offers free sledding hills with multiple runs for different thrill levels.

**What to know:**

**Cost:** FREE (sleds ~$15-30 at local stores)

### 5. üé£ Try Gold Medal Fishing

**Best for:** Ages 5+ | Year-round (best in summer)

**Kid-friendly options:**

### 6. üö£ Go Tubing on the San Juan River

- Tube rentals available at several downtown shops
- Great way to cool off after hiking

**Safety note:** Water levels vary ‚Äî check conditions before going, and life jackets are recommended for kids.

**Cost:** Tube rental ~$15-20/day
