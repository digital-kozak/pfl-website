# Weekend in Pagosa Springs: 2-Day Escape Plan

*Print this guide or save it to your phone for your quick mountain getaway*

---

## Overview

Only have a weekend? No problem. This efficient 2-day itinerary packs the best of Pagosa into 48 hours — hot springs, great food, mountain views, and unique experiences. It's designed to maximize your limited time while still feeling relaxed.

**Best For:** Couples, friends, quick escapes from city life  
**Season:** Year-round  
**Base:** [Pagosa Forest Lodge](https://pagosaforestlodge.com/the-cabin) — your private retreat

---

## Friday Evening: Arrival & Unwind

### 6:00 PM — Check In & Explore

Arrive at Pagosa Forest Lodge and settle into your 4-bedroom cabin:
- Drop your bags and explore the property
- Pour a glass of wine or make a cup of tea
- Step onto the deck and take in the mountain views
- Light the fire pit (if weather permits)

### 7:30 PM — Dinner in Town

**Option 1: Casual — Riff Raff Brewing**
- Local craft beer and elevated pub food
- Try the Trout Creek Amber (their flagship)
- Green Chile Cheeseburger is a must
- Dog-friendly patio

**Option 2: Upscale — Meander Riverside Eatery**
- Reservations recommended
- San Juan River views
- Perfect for a special weekend
- Colorado lamb or fresh seafood

**Option 3: Quick — Tequila's Mexican**
- Fast, delicious, affordable
- Green chile smothered everything
- Margaritas by the pitcher

### 9:30 PM — Private Hot Tub

End your evening with a soak in your private hot tub:
- Stargazing (Gold-Tier Dark Sky!)
- Mountain views
- Complete privacy
- No closing time — soak until you're pruney

---

## Saturday: Adventure Day

### 8:00 AM — Breakfast

**Option 1: At the Cabin**
- Cook in the fully equipped kitchen
- Coffee on the deck with mountain views
- No rush, no crowds

**Option 2: The Junction Restaurant**
- Pagosa institution since forever
- Chicken fried steak (mountain-sized)
- Biscuits and gravy
- Classic diner experience

### 9:30 AM — Choose Your Morning Adventure

**Option A: Scenic Drive (All Seasons)**
- **Wolf Creek Pass** — 30 minutes to summit
- Stop at Treasure Falls (0.8-mile hike)
- Views into New Mexico
- Back by lunch

**Option B: Hot Springs Morning (All Seasons)**
- **The Springs Resort** — Arrive at opening
- Beat the crowds
- Try 5-6 different pools
- Relax until noon

**Option C: Skiing (Winter Only)**
- **Wolf Creek Ski Area** — Leave by 8:30 AM
- 40-minute drive
- "Most Snow in Colorado"
- Ski 9 AM - 1 PM

**Option D: Hiking (Spring/Summer/Fall)**
- **Reservoir Hill** — Easy, downtown
- **Treasure Falls** — Short hike, big reward
- **Fourmile Falls** — Moderate, 5 miles

### 12:30 PM — Lunch

**Quick Options:**
- **Square Top BBQ** (Thurs-Sun only) — Brisket and pulled pork
- **Pagosa Baking Company** — Sandwiches and pastries
- **Kip's Grill** — Fish tacos on the patio
- **Boss Hoggs** — Burgers and sandwiches

### 2:00 PM — Afternoon Activity

**Option A: Downtown Exploration**
- Gallery hop (Main Street Gallery, Artist's Gallery)
- Shopping (boutiques, souvenirs)
- Coffee at Root House or Bruin
- People-watching

**Option B: Second Hot Springs Visit**
- **Overlook Hot Springs** — Rooftop tubs
- Different vibe from The Springs Resort
- Victorian ambiance
- San Juan River views

**Option C: Chimney Rock National Monument**
- 45-minute drive
- 2-3 hours to explore
- Ancient Puebloan ruins
- 1,000+ years of history
- Back by dinner

**Option D: Relax at the Cabin**
- Nap in the mountain air
- Read on the deck
- Soak in the hot tub
- Do absolutely nothing

### 6:00 PM — Dinner

**Saturday Night Options:**

**Casual:**
- **The Break Room Brewing** — Craft beer, late hours
- **Rosie's Pizzeria** — NY-style pizza
- **Dublin Crust & Brew** — Pizza and beer

**Nice:**
- **Alley House Grill** — Steak and seafood
- **Gustoso** — Italian cuisine
- **Kip's Grill** — Mexican cantina atmosphere

**Special:**
- **Meander Riverside Eatery** — If you didn't go Friday
- **Wild Finch @ Springs Resort** — Elegant dining

### 8:30 PM — Evening Entertainment

**Option 1: Live Music**
- Check Riff Raff or Break Room for weekend music
- Local and regional acts
- Casual, fun atmosphere

**Option 2: Nighttime Hot Springs**
- The Springs Resort is open late
- Soaking under the stars
- Different experience than daytime

**Option 3: Cabin Time**
- Fire pit and conversation
- Board games (bring your favorites)
- Hot tub and stargazing
- Early night for Sunday adventures

---

## Sunday: Relaxed Departure

### 8:00 AM — Leisurely Breakfast

**Option 1: Two Chicks & A Hippy**
- Creative breakfast dishes
- House-made pastries
- Quirky, charming atmosphere

**Option 2: Patria**
- Farm-to-table breakfast
- Locally sourced ingredients
- Elevated classics

**Option 3: At the Cabin**
- Cook together
- No rush to check out
- Enjoy the mountain morning

### 9:30 AM — Morning Activity

**Option A: Easy Hike**
- **Reservoir Hill** — 1-2 hours
- Disc golf course
- Downtown location
- Great views

**Option B: Final Hot Springs Soak**
- **Healing Waters** — Budget-friendly
- 100% natural mineral water
- Less crowded on Sunday morning
- Relaxing way to end the trip

**Option C: Farmers Market (Summer Only)**
- Saturdays, 9 AM - 1 PM
- Downtown, next to the river
- Local produce and crafts
- Perfect for snacks for the road

**Option D: Sleep In + Coffee**
- Skip the morning activity
- Sleep until you wake naturally
- Coffee on the deck
- Check out at 11 AM

### 11:00 AM — Check Out

- Pack up
- Take final photos of the cabin and views
- Load the car
- Stop by the office if you need anything

### 11:30 AM — Brunch Before You Go

**Sunday Brunch Options:**

**Ramon's Mexican**
- Sunday brunch menu
- Mexican-American favorites
- Mimosas and Bloody Marys

**The Rose**
- Weekend brunch (expect a wait)
- Farm-to-table
- Fresh pastries

**Cafe Colorado**
- Breakfast served late
- Hearty portions
- Casual atmosphere

### 1:00 PM — Final Stop

**Before leaving town:**

**Option 1: Quick Souvenirs**
- Pagosa Souvenirs & Gifts
- The Hot Springs Store
- Local coffee beans to take home

**Option 2: One Last Look**
- Riverwalk downtown
- Reservoir Hill overlook
- Final mountain views

**Option 3: Hit the Road**
- Start your drive home
- Plan your next visit!

## Quick Reference

### Drive Times from Cabin
- Downtown: 15 minutes
- The Springs Resort: 15 minutes
- Wolf Creek Ski: 40 minutes
- Chimney Rock: 45 minutes
- Treasure Falls: 25 minutes

### Must-Make Reservations
- **Meander Riverside Eatery:** 2-3 days ahead
- **Wolf Creek ski lessons:** Day before
- **Spa treatments:** Day before

### What to Pack for a Weekend
- Layers (weather changes)
- Swimsuit (hot springs!)
- Comfortable shoes
- Nice outfit (for Saturday dinner)
- Rain jacket (just in case)
